## The Problem (Raw Version)

The user isn't **just stressed or anxious**. They're **emotionally dysregulated** — their nervous system reacts before they can think, they feel overwhelmed by triggers they can't identify, and they carry emotional weight that affects everything.

Somewhere along the way, their nervous system got calibrated to threat mode. Maybe it was childhood stress, difficult relationships, or accumulated life pressure. Now their body keeps score even when their mind tries to move on.

Now they're here. Tired but wired. Reactive. Cycling through emotions they can't name or control. They've tried meditation apps (too passive), therapy (too expensive, too slow), journaling on their own (no structure, no support).

They've tried willpower. They've tried "just calm down." None of it worked because:

**They're not lacking willpower. Their nervous system is hijacking their responses.**

## Emotional Funnel

### Level 1: Pain (Present)
Landing page opens with "Life comes without a manual" — acknowledges that life is hard to navigate. Feature cards address specific pains:
- Tired/lack energy
- Overwhelmed by tasks
- Mood swings
- Difficulty expressing emotions

**Assessment:** Pain is present but spread across features rather than concentrated in opening.

### Level 2: Recognition (Strong)
The quiz funnel (26 questions) creates deep recognition:
- "How often do you feel worried or overwhelmed?"
- "Do you often leave things to the last minute?"
- "Have you struggled with accepting compliments?"

This surfaces patterns users may not have consciously named.

### Level 3: Reframe (Implicit)
The "science-backed" and "nervous system" framing implies reframe: it's your wiring, not your character.

The Expert section ("Backed by science and focused on solutions") reinforces: credentialed professionals validate this isn't your fault.

### Level 4: Validation (Strong)
- Testimonials: "This app helped me understand my emotions"
- Social proof: "2,500,000+ people chose Liven"
- Expert endorsements: Licensed therapists, health coaches

### Level 5: Solution (Very Strong)
Clear product features, personalized plan promise, structured program, daily engagement model. The 26-question quiz creates high investment before solution reveal.

**Assessment:** Full emotional funnel is present, though pain acknowledgment could be stronger at entry.

## Opportunities for New Product

### What to Learn From Liven
- Quiz funnel with 20+ questions creates deep investment
- "Nervous system" framing is credible and trending
- AI companion adds differentiation and 24/7 value
- Social proof at scale builds trust
- Expert endorsement adds authority
- Multiple features create perceived value

### What to Improve Upon
- Create more unique proprietary language (not just "nervous system")
- Develop deeper emotional entry point (Memento's product brief had better pain articulation)
- Combine Liven's scale with Memento's depth
- Add progress tracking and transformation visualization
- Create community/social features

### Hybrid Opportunity
Combine:
- **Memento's depth:** Self-abandonment patterns, identity reconnection, literary copy
- **Liven's scale:** Sophisticated funnel, social proof, AI companion
- **Best of both:** Structured chapters + daily engagement + personalization
